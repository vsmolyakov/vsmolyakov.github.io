Vadim Smolyakov

Data Scientist

Microsoft

I am a Data Scientist at Microsoft. I was a PhD student in the Sensing, Learning, and Inference (SLI) lab at MIT CSAIL.

My research interests are in Bayesian inference, probabilistic modeling and deep learning in application to computer vision and natural language processing.

I completed my MASc degree specializing in wireless communications and BASc degree in Engineering Science at the University of Toronto

In the past, I interned as a Data Scientist at Microsoft, Rue Gilt Groupe and Shopify. I was also at Qualcomm and Max Linear as a systems research intern and a digital baseband designer.
